Project Readiness Checklist

Preparing the following details helps us provide a faster and more accurate proposal for your project scope.

Business and Operational Inputs

  • Primary business objective and expected operational outcome.
  • Current workflow pain points and measurable bottlenecks.
  • Project timeline expectations and target launch window.
  • Stakeholder structure and decision-making process.

Technical and Compliance Inputs

  • Existing systems, infrastructure environment, and integration boundaries.
  • Mobile app requirements for Google Play and Apple App Store.
  • Privacy, age, advertising, and regional policy considerations.
  • Support model preference: phased delivery or full program rollout.
